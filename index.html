What we do

Abhyunnatti Bhaswa is a young non-profit organization run by social advocates who are dedicated in securing a brighter future for underprivileged children in India.

Our mission is to build a solid foundation for these children that allow them to climb the socio-economic ladder by offering support with financial management, funds and grant applications, educational opportunities, healthcare resources and more. Our volunteers consult with local managers of orphanages, homes and shelters to secure these children with important basic rights. We help our beneficiaries develop relationships with for-profit corporations on the basis of social responsibility.

Children born at a disadvantage don’t deserve to be left behind and we are here to look out for them.

Open Volunteer Positions

We here at ABF volunteer our time, experience and expertise not out of expectation of recognition or compensation but out of dedication to be a contributive member of society that work tirelessly to support those in need with the skills we have acquired. We are always looking to add new members who believe in our cause to our team.

As a development consultant, you can support local social workers and entrepreneurs with sustainable solutions for the health, educational and financial needs of underprivileged children by finding and creating opportunities for a higher standard of living.

We seek self-motivated, innovative, emotionally intelligent individuals with excellent interpersonal skills that not only have a knack for finding suitable opportunities for growth but also be able to create opportunities with their creative thinking.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • As a development consultant, your main role would be to find innovative ways of solving problems faced by social workers and social entrepreneurs that shelter and support underprivileged children.
  • In your role, you will research and find specific resources to cater to the educational, health and financial needs of children.
  • You can collaborate with fellow volunteers to start new programs that support the all-round development and growth of children to give them a bright future.
  • You will negotiate and maintain relationships with for-profit organisations on the basis of CSR to provide higher quality of resources for children.

As a Field Scout at ABF, your main roles would be to work on the field, scout for small-scale unprivileged children homes, orphanages, shelters, social entrepreneurs and social workers that would benefit from our services, and conduct preliminary and detailed surveys to determine the nature of their requirements. This profile will require the ability to promote ABF's work and services as well as equip the organization with comprehensive information about the units that require service.

We are seeking self-motivated individuals with a passion for community service, a tireless work ethic, proactivity in taking initiative and excellent interpersonal skills.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Conducting field surveys with small and medium target units.
  • Preparing reports based on market research.
  • Representing the organisation's goals and interests with target groups.
  • Promoting organsation's causes with target units.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior experience in the public relations/ marketing/ non-profit sector.
  • Demonstrated ability in oral and written communication skills.
  • Time commitment of 4-6 hours per week.
  • Ability to speak regional language as well as English.
